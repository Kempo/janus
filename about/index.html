Why did Janus start?

To address a prevalent, critical wound in the privacy industry today.

Here's the fuel for why Janus should exist.

Your data is extremely valuable, but you aren't rewarded for it.

Your data is unprotected, and is stolen from you.

This is wrong.

Instead of this, there should be a mutually benefitting relationship between user and company.

Users should...

  1. get rewarded for the use of their information
  2. rightfully protect their data
  3. control who gets to access their information

Be empowered with your data using Janus.

A browser extension that does data your way.
